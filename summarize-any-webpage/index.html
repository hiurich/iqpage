About summarizing webpages with IQPage.

Yes. IQPage works on any website with text content — news articles, research papers, blogs, Wikipedia entries, GitHub READMEs, and more. If there's text on the page, IQPage can summarize it in under 10 seconds.

Under 10 seconds for most pages. IQPage reads the full page text, sends it to Claude AI (Anthropic), and returns a structured summary with executive summary, key points, and main takeaway.

The free plan includes 3 summaries per week. Edu ($5/mo) gives 80/month, Pro ($12/mo) gives 200/month, and Power ($29/mo) gives unlimited summaries. All plans reset automatically.

IQPage reads what's visible in your browser. If you have access to the full article (subscription, login), IQPage will summarize the full content. For paywalled content you can't access, it will summarize what's available.
